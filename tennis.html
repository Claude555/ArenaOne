Tennis

Game . Set . Match

Tennis is a popular game amongst many in our region. So we come up with a high quality court at an affordable price for all. We offer a number of programs;

  • Tennis academy: Let your kid learn to play basketball like a pro.
  • Tennis Tournaments: We offer monthly and seasonal competitions.
  • Tennis Tournament: Pick your best 3 players to compete.

The tournament is an initiative of Arena One, to not only organize a regular tournament but to add value to each participating player/team and promote sports participation and fitness. Our theme “Active Life=Healthy Life” is heavily in line with the overall health and wellness during the COVID-19 pandemic
