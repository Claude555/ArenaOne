Programs Offered.

We have a vast variety of options for YOU.

BasketBall Academy

A1 Basketball camps and clinics are great for improving specific skills. Every one of our highly skilled and experienced trainers are equipped to improve your game in all aspects; Players will learn proper shooting technique, footwork, dribbling, defence, physical and mental toughness and how to play multiple positions

Arena One Academy basketball camps are available monthly for boys and girls ages 4-19, with programs for everyone from the novice athlete to the aspiring pro. The Camp program includes oncourt group sessions that focus on shooting, dribbling, defence, and more. All basketball attendees receive a post-camp analysis report inclusive of coach feedback and important takeaways.

Arena One aspires to bring affordable class leading sporting facilities to all Kenyans in a bid to maximise our potential, productively channel our societies’ energies and ultimately uplift communities​.

ARENA ONE FOOTBALL ACADEMY

Arena One Football Academy offers technical development and competition in a safe, sun environment with an emphasis on teamwork, discipline, a strong work ethic and respect.

Soccer Clinic: Our monthly Clinics are open to any and all boys and girls ages 4-13. Each day begins with dynamic warm up exercises to loosen up and get ready for the day. This is usually followed by the technical portion of the session, focusing on ball control, passing and shooting. The Saturday and Sunday sessions conclude with small sided fun games where players will have the opportunity to utilize skills learned during the clinic session

ARENAONE & NIVEA CORPORATE TOURNAMENT

At Arena One, we host and design amazing football and basketball competitions for our clients. The corporate friendly is an exciting way for companies to benefit in some team building and networking opportunities in a competitive and energetic environment


A1 OPEN TOURNAMENT

The tournament is an initiative of Arena One, to not only organize a regular tournament but to add value to each participating player/team and promote sports participation and fitness. Our theme “Active Life=Healthy Life” is heavily in line with the overall health and wellness during the COVID-19 pandemic.

BASKETBALL 3X3 TOURNAMENT (OPEN/WOMEN)

3X3 is known for being highly entertaining and has been recently introduced as an Olympic sport. We host monthly 3x3 tournaments that are popular and attract amateur and professional teams from all over Nairobi. Our aim is to grow the game of basketball in the community by providing athletes of all levels the opportunity and experience of world class products, services, and facilities
